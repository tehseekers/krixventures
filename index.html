What We Trade

Our Product Range

We trade a comprehensive range of stainless steel products to meet diverse industrial requirements, sourced from certified mills worldwide.

HR Coils

Hot rolled stainless steel coils in various grades including 304, 316, and 430, suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications.

CR Coils

Cold rolled coils with superior surface finish and tighter tolerances, ideal for precision manufacturing and consumer goods.

Sheets & Plates

Flat stainless steel sheets and plates in multiple thicknesses and finishes for construction, architecture, and fabrication.

Pipes & Tubes

Seamless and welded stainless steel pipes and tubes for fluid transport, structural support, and industrial piping systems.

Coil Slitting

Custom slit coils to precise widths, enabling efficient material usage and reducing waste for downstream manufacturers.

Bars & Rods

Round, flat, and hexagonal stainless steel bars for machining, fasteners, and structural applications across industries.
